About

February 21, 2018 · 2 min · Bart de Goede

Hi! My name is Bart de Goede. I live in New York City and I’m the Engineering Manager for Search & Discovery at StubHub, helping find tickets to their favorite live events.

I’ve also been a member of the technical staff at Perplexity AI, building the most powerful knowledge tool on the web, search engineering lead at OnFrontiers, where I helped organizations understand the knowledge and expertise their employees accrue, a software engineer at Instacart, on the search team at Scribd, and an engineer on the search and data science team at Catawiki. I’ve also run my own business helping other companies build search engines.

For the complete professional bio, please refer to my LinkedIn profile or my resume.

Besides tinkering with software, I enjoy traveling, eating (nice) food, playing D&D or going for a drink with friends.

Most of the posts I write are about things related to tech and programming, or anything else I find interesting.

Find me on GitHub, LinkedIn or Twitter.
